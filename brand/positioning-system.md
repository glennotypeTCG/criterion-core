# Brand Positioning System

## Purpose

The brand system defines how Criterion Group, Fetch Happens, Axiom Foundry, SwiftVC, and NewCos are explained to the market without fragmenting the operating truth.

The brand system should translate the operating system for different audiences. It should not invent separate stories.

## Master Narrative

Criterion Group turns validated market signals into venture-backable companies.

Fetch Happens tests demand before build. Axiom Foundry forms companies around the strongest signals. SwiftVC invests first into qualified opportunities. NewCos leave the Foundry as independent startups that must prove themselves in the market.

## Entity Positioning

| Entity | Category | Clean Positioning |
|---|---|---|
| Criterion Group | Company-formation system | The operating system for turning domain insight into venture-backable companies. |
| Fetch Happens | Market signal engine | Tests whether customers care before anyone overbuilds. |
| Axiom Foundry | Company formation layer | Turns positive-signal ideas into structured, fundable companies. |
| SwiftVC | Embedded first-check capital layer | Invests first into companies formed through the Criterion system. |
| NewCos | Independent startups | Formed companies that must prove demand, delivery, and revenue capture in the market. |

## Audience Translations

### Idea Person / Founder

Criterion helps turn your insight into a real company by testing demand, forming the company, and creating a path to first capital.

### LP / Capital Partner

Criterion offers exposure to companies formed through a proprietary signal-first operating system rather than open-market pre-seed sourcing alone.

### Follow-On Investor

Criterion delivers cleaner early-stage companies with stronger signal, better formation discipline, and clearer investor narratives.

### Strategic Partner

Criterion creates and supports companies around validated market problems, with a structured path from signal to formation to commercialization.

### Internal Operator

Fetch signal, form companies, fund selectively, and compound learning across the portfolio.

## Approved One-Liners

- Criterion Group manufactures better early-stage startup conditions.
- Criterion turns validated market signals into venture-backable companies.
- Fetch Happens validates demand before build.
- Axiom Foundry converts signal into structure.
- SwiftVC invests before the market has access.
- NewCos must earn their independence in the market.

## Forbidden or High-Risk Language

Avoid language that implies:

- guaranteed returns
- guaranteed venture outcomes
- guaranteed fundraising success
- automatic investment after validation
- mandatory services as fee extraction
- that Fetch validation alone proves venture-backability
- that Axiom permanently operates the company
- that SwiftVC eliminates venture risk

## Brand Rule

The brand promise must always map back to an operating truth, an evidence standard, and an economic reason.
