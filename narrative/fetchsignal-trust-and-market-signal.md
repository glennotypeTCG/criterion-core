# FetchSignal Trust and Market Signal Narrative

## Purpose

This file is the canonical narrative for FetchSignal / Fetch Happens when speaking to an Idea Principal before they have entered any later formation or capital process.

Use it when explaining why an Idea Principal should trust FetchSignal to test an idea with real market participants before the idea becomes a company, before Axiom Foundry is introduced, and before SwiftVC is relevant.

This language should guide:

- FetchSignal website copy
- Idea Principal intake flows
- Discovery sprint positioning
- Interview consent language
- Market signal report framing
- Referral language after a study
- Future ontology or MCP references around independent signal generation

This file should be read alongside:

- `narrative/swiftvc-lp-point-of-view.md`
- `narrative/swiftvc-dual-close-idea-principal.md`

## Public Brand Rule

**Criterion is not a public-facing brand, customer promise, or formal ecosystem that FetchSignal customers need to understand.**

Internally, Criterion may describe the broader system that includes FetchSignal, Axiom Foundry, SwiftVC, and other related capabilities.

Externally, FetchSignal should stand on its own as the trusted market-signal brand.

Do not tell FetchSignal customers that they are entering the Criterion ecosystem.

Do not imply that FetchSignal automatically leads to Axiom Foundry, SwiftVC, or any internal Criterion-affiliated next step.

Instead, explain that FetchSignal can help the Idea Principal understand what the market says and, where appropriate, introduce relevant next-step resources.

Those resources may include:

- Axiom Foundry
- SwiftVC
- Independent legal providers
- Formation tools
- Accounting providers
- Product development partners
- Grant, lender, or small-business resources
- Marketplaces or software tools
- Services like LegalZoom or similar business-formation providers

Internally, there may be a preference to refer strong-fit opportunities to Axiom Foundry and SwiftVC.

Externally, referrals should be framed as optional next-step resources, not as a required or guaranteed path.

A referral, preferred provider introduction, or discount code does **not** mean FetchSignal must share the full idea, the full study, customer interview recordings, or detailed results with that provider.

The Idea Principal controls what is shared, with whom, and when, subject to any explicit written permissions or agreements.

## Canonical Point of View

> **Most good ideas stay trapped inside trusted circles because sharing them feels risky. FetchSignal gives an Idea Principal a safer way to let the market touch the idea without making it feel less theirs.**

## What Has to Be True

> **For FetchSignal to work, the Idea Principal has to believe that controlled, respectful conversations with real ICPs can produce a signal they trust, without exposing the idea carelessly, damaging their reputation, or limiting what they can do next.**

## Emotional Center

The deepest question is not, "Will this research be useful?"

The deeper question is:

> **Do I trust you enough to let my idea leave my head?**

For many Idea Principals, the idea may feel like one of the most valuable things they have.

That is why they may have kept it close. They may have shared it only with a spouse, trusted friend, co-founder candidate, advisor, or close family member. They may worry that outside feedback will expose the idea, weaken it, embarrass them, or make it feel less theirs.

That fear is not irrational.

It is one of the reasons many good ideas never get real market feedback.

FetchSignal exists to create a safer alternative.

> **You do not have to choose between hiding your idea and overexposing it.**

---

## Human Version

> **Your idea may be valuable. That is exactly why it should not be shared casually, tested sloppily, or kept hidden forever. FetchSignal gives your idea a controlled first contact with the market. We help you learn whether real buyers care, while protecting the idea, your reputation, and your choice about what happens next.**

The real promise is not just independent validation.

> **The real promise is safe first contact with the market.**

## Safety Promise

FetchSignal must be safe across five dimensions.

| Safety Dimension | What the Idea Principal Needs to Believe |
|---|---|
| Idea safety | The idea will not be stolen, leaked, casually broadcast, or exposed more than necessary. |
| Reputation safety | The outreach will not make the Idea Principal look amateur, spammy, desperate, or unserious. |
| Emotional safety | A negative signal will not be treated as embarrassment or failure. |
| Strategic safety | The process will preserve future options, not burn bridges or poison the market. |
| Ownership safety | Testing the idea will not make it feel less theirs. |

The standard:

> **We do not expose your idea casually. We test it deliberately.**

---

## Neural Revenue Architecture

FetchSignal conversion is not rational-only. It follows a human trust sequence.

| Stage | Customer Feeling | Required Message |
|---|---|---|
| Safety | My idea is safe here. | We test carefully, not casually. |
| Legibility | I understand how this works. | You can see the process before committing. |
| Credibility | The signal will mean something. | Real ICPs, structured conversations, independent synthesis. |
| Agency | I still decide what happens next. | You control next steps and what gets shared. |
| Pathway | This can create momentum if the signal is strong. | The signal can support decisions, referrals, partners, investors, or formation paths. |

Internal shorthand:

> **Safe → Clear → Credible → Yours → Next**

## What the Customer Has to Believe

The FetchSignal customer has to believe two things.

### 1. The Signal Is Worth Getting

> **I will be better off knowing what the market says than continuing to protect the idea from evidence.**

A negative signal is not failure. It is avoided waste.

A positive signal is not guaranteed success. It means the idea has earned the right to be taken more seriously.

### 2. FetchSignal Is the Right Team to Get It

> **If I am going to let anyone take this idea outside my trusted circle, I trust FetchSignal to do it more safely, more professionally, and more independently than I could do on my own.**

That is the buying decision.

---

## ICP Conversation Belief

The customer does not need to believe every ICP will respond or that every conversation will be perfect.

They need to believe this:

> **A structured set of human-led, AI-supported conversations can reveal patterns that are more reliable than founder intuition alone.**

The purpose is not to broadcast the idea.

The purpose is to learn what the market is willing to tell us.

---

## The Signal Should Travel

FetchSignal output should be credible enough to support the next serious conversation.

The signal may help the Idea Principal speak with:

- A spouse or family member
- A co-founder candidate
- An advisor
- A first employee
- A customer or design partner
- A lender, grant reviewer, or small-business resource where relevant
- An angel investor
- A venture investor
- A product development partner
- A formation provider
- Axiom Foundry, if appropriate
- SwiftVC, if the idea fits the narrow capital path

FetchSignal should not promise that the study will guarantee financing, admission to a formation process, or investment.

Preferred language:

> **FetchSignal does not guarantee capital. It creates evidence strong enough to help the next serious person understand why the idea deserves more attention, or why it should change before more time and money are spent.**

---

## Ecosystem and Referral Bridge

Do not lead with SwiftVC.

Most FetchSignal customers will not receive SwiftVC capital.

Do not lead with Axiom Foundry.

Most FetchSignal customers may need other next steps before formation, or they may decide to build independently.

Instead:

> **If the signal is strong, you may have more paths: build it yourself, recruit a co-founder, refine the idea, take the evidence to another investor, use a service provider, or come back to FetchSignal for help identifying next-step resources.**

Where appropriate, FetchSignal may make referrals to internal or external resources.

Those referrals do not require full disclosure of the idea or study unless the Idea Principal chooses to share that information.

A discount code, marketplace link, or provider introduction should not be confused with permission to share confidential study materials.

## Messaging Guardrails

Do say:

- FetchSignal helps your idea safely meet the market.
- The process is controlled, deliberate, and respectful.
- The goal is evidence, not exposure.
- The Idea Principal controls what happens next.
- A strong signal can open options, but it does not guarantee funding.
- A referral does not require sharing the full idea or study.
- FetchSignal can refer to both independent third parties and affiliated next-step resources where appropriate.

Do not say:

- You are entering the Criterion ecosystem.
- FetchSignal guarantees SwiftVC funding.
- FetchSignal guarantees Axiom Foundry formation support.
- We will prove your idea is good.
- You must share your full idea with every referral partner.
- A discount code or referral means your full study will be shared.
- Negative signal means failure.
- We are just doing customer interviews.

Preferred terms:

- Safe first contact with the market
- Controlled market test
- Independent signal
- Real ICP conversations
- Brand-safe outreach
- Deliberate testing
- Next-step resources
- Optional referrals
- Customer-controlled sharing

Avoid terms:

- Public ecosystem
- Automatic funnel
- Guaranteed funding path
- Idea exposure
- Broadcasting the idea
- Criterion customer journey
- Mandatory referral
