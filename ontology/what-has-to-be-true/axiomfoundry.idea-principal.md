# Axiom Foundry: What Has to Be True

## Ontology Type

what-has-to-be-true

## Entity

Axiom Foundry

## Primary Stakeholder

Idea Principal

## Canonical Belief

> For the dual close to work, the Idea Principal has to believe Axiom can help turn the idea into a more valuable company than they could create alone, fast enough and clearly enough to justify the ownership and structure.

## Plain-English Version

Axiom works if a formed, fundable company feels more valuable than an under-formed idea built alone.

## Emotional Job

Make disciplined formation feel protective and empowering rather than controlling.

## Functional Job

Convert validated signal into a venture-backable company through structured formation, operating cadence, and disciplined capital use.

## Proof Required

- Faster movement from signal to company
- Reduced avoidable waste
- Clear operating cadence
- Investor-readiness support
- Formation discipline that increases company value

## Failure Mode

The Idea Principal experiences the structure as control extraction rather than value creation.

## Messaging Guardrails

### Do Say

- Formation offer
- Operating partner
- Six-month formation process
- Disciplined company formation
- Capital plus formation support

### Do Not Say

- Axiom controls the company
- Forced spend
- Mandatory consulting
- Giving up control
- The founder could never do this alone
