# Criterion Group: What Has to Be True

## Ontology Type

what-has-to-be-true

## Entity

Criterion Group

## Primary Stakeholder

System stakeholder: Idea Principals, LPs, aligned capital relationships, formation relationships, referral relationships, and internal operators.

## Canonical Belief

> Criterion Group exists if coordinated sequencing creates structural advantage: test the idea safely, fund only when signal justifies it, and form the company with enough discipline to improve its odds before the market has priced the opportunity.

## Plain-English Version

Criterion Group exists because good ideas need more than feedback, money, or help in isolation. They need the right sequence: evidence first, capital when earned, and disciplined formation when the opportunity is real.

## Emotional Job

Make the system feel coherent without making it feel controlling, automatic, or predatory.

## Functional Job

Coordinate distinct capabilities so that independent signal, formation capital, and disciplined company-building support work better together than they would separately.

## Proof Required

- Shared standards across signal, capital, and formation
- Clear permissioning and data-sharing boundaries
- Evidence that signal improves capital and formation decisions
- Evidence that capital-backed formation improves company quality
- Clear governance across distinct entities and relationships

## Failure Mode

Criterion becomes a branding wrapper around disconnected services, or worse, creates legal, trust, or governance confusion by implying common ownership, automatic referrals, or guaranteed funding where none exists.

## Core Beliefs Required

1. Formation risk is real.
2. Signal alone is not enough.
3. Capital alone is not enough.
4. Formation support alone is not enough.
5. Coordination creates advantage.

## Messaging Guardrails

### Do Say

- Coordination architecture
- Shared standards
- Sequenced capabilities
- Distinct entities
- Permission-based relationships
- Structural advantage before the market sees the opportunity

### Do Not Say

- Holding company, unless legally true
- Public ecosystem
- Guaranteed funnel
- Automatic referral
- Common ownership, unless legally true
- Criterion controls SwiftVC
- FetchSignal data is shared by default
