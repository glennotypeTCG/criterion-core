# FetchSignal: What Has to Be True

## Ontology Type

what-has-to-be-true

## Entity

FetchSignal / Fetch Happens

## Primary Stakeholder

Idea Principal

## Canonical Belief

> For FetchSignal to work, the Idea Principal has to believe that controlled, respectful conversations with real ICPs can produce a signal they trust, without exposing the idea carelessly, damaging their reputation, or limiting what they can do next.

## Plain-English Version

The Idea Principal must trust FetchSignal to take the idea outside their head without making it less safe, less credible, or less theirs.

## Emotional Job

Create safe first contact with the market.

## Functional Job

Produce credible independent market signal before major company formation or capital deployment.

## Proof Required

- Real ICP conversations
- Structured signal synthesis
- Brand-safe outreach
- Controlled information sharing
- Customer-controlled next steps

## Failure Mode

The Idea Principal fears exposure more than they value the signal.

## Messaging Guardrails

### Do Say

- Safe first contact with the market
- Controlled market test
- Independent signal
- Brand-safe outreach
- The customer controls what happens next

### Do Not Say

- Guaranteed funding path
- Public ecosystem
- Broadcasting the idea
- Automatic funnel into SwiftVC or Axiom Foundry
