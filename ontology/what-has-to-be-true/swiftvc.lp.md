# SwiftVC: What Has to Be True

## Ontology Type

what-has-to-be-true

## Entity

SwiftVC

## Primary Stakeholder

LP

## Canonical Belief

> For SwiftVC to work, early signal has to lead to better formation decisions, better companies, and enough early ownership for LPs to benefit from the difference.

## Plain-English Version

SwiftVC works if signal-before-pitch creates companies worth owning before the market sees them clearly.

## Emotional Job

Help LPs believe venture advantage can exist before the company looks conventionally investable.

## Functional Job

Convert validated signal and disciplined formation into fund-relevant ownership and venture outcomes.

## Proof Required

- Better formation quality
- Disciplined filtering
- Meaningful retained ownership
- Companies shaped before the broader market has access
- Venture-scale upside potential

## Failure Mode

Signal does not materially improve company quality or SwiftVC fails to retain enough ownership for the advantage to matter.

## Messaging Guardrails

### Do Say

- Earlier than the pitch
- Independent signal
- Disciplined formation
- Better starting conditions for venture-backed companies
- Proprietary formation exposure

### Do Not Say

- Traditional venture is broken for everyone
- SwiftVC is smarter than the market
- Signal eliminates venture risk
- Every validated idea deserves capital
