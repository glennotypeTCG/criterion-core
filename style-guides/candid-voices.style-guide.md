# Candid Voices Style Guide

## Status

Placeholder

## Purpose

This Style Guide will govern voice-of-customer, interview, recording, and conversational research materials under the Candid Voices concept.

## Applies To

- Recorded conversations
- Founder interviews
- Voice-of-customer content
- Research and storytelling assets
- Build-night and conversational media

## Current Rule

Candid Voices should feel:

- Human
- Curious
- Warm
- Thoughtful
- Honest
- Respectful
- Conversational

It should not feel:

- Like surveillance
- Like a podcast bro brand
- Like manufactured authenticity
- Like aggressive sales
- Like exploitative founder content
